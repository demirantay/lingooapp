Why Domingo?

Domingo's topmost priority is its community. We believe that language learning should not be a solo adventure. That is why most of our learning content is towards people interacting with each other while learning a new language.

Grow Your Vocabulary Tree

With our fun vocabulary course lessons, you will be able to learn up to 8,000-16,000 words in your target language. We take our vocabulary lessons very seriously because it is one of the most important milestones on your journey to becoming fluent in another language.

Track Your Progress

We know that one of the hardest parts about learning a new language is to keep learning without losing your motivation. That is why we track and show nearly all of your learning statistics so that you can motivate yourself about your progress.

Your Voice Matters

We don't throw the "community-driven" phrase to just catch your eye. We believe that this community must have its own voice on how the platform should be run. Every user in this platform gets voting rights that have the ability to add or remove features that the community wants.
