Why Domingo?

Domingo's top most priorty is it's community. We beleive that language learning should not be a solo adventure. That is why most of our learning content is geared towards people interacting with each other and learning a new language.

Grow Your Vocabulary Tree

With our fun vocabulary course lessons you will be able to learn up to 8,000-16,000 words in your target language. We take our vocabulary lessons very seriously because it is one of the most important milestone on your journey to becomeing fluent in another language.

Track Your Progress

We know that one of the hardest part about learning a new language is to keep learning without losing your motivation. That is why we track and show nearly all of your statistics of learning so that you can learn and motivate yourself about your progress.

Your Voice Matters

We don't throw the "community-driven" phrase to just catch your eye. We believe that every community on the internet must have their own voice on how that platform should be run. Every user in this platform gets voting rights that has the ability to add or remove features that the community does or does not want.
