About Us

Our mission as Lingoo family is to teach languages to people who would like to learn a second or a third language. Everybody has a different reason to learn a new language. Maybe you are trying to move to a different country, get a promotion at work ... or maybe you just enjoy learning languages as a hobby! of it!

Don't worry! no matter what is your purpose, we got you covered. Once you get past the A1-A2 levels, you will start to see that the learning tree will get personalized as you go along. Why do we do this and not teach you all of the languages? Simple, because even though C1 leveled people are considered fluent they really lack a specific jargon of words. You simply cannot learn every word out there!

So we use a survival-of-the-fittest strategy while teaching you the language. If you are interested in history, by the time you are done with your learning tree, you will know a lot of history terminology rather than medicine, engineering, and ... etc.

Our Community Centric Approach

We believe that at some point nearly most of the companies out there lose touch with their community once they get big enough. For us, this is a huge loss to the community of people who spend their hours on those platforms. That is why we are designing our company's decision mechanism from bottom-to-up.

What does that mean? It means that the users will be included in every decision about the features of the product. No, we won't get your advice on how to structure our financial reports but we will ALWAYS get your input on feature decisions.

We believe that when people become part of a community, every single person should get a voice. That is why we have a voting ballot system implemented within the site where we hold referendums when we want to get input from our users. Also, we will implement a senate system where users will be able to create their feature requests and those features will always have the up-most priority over any other feature.
